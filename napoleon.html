LÉONE WELCH

LAST WORKS ARCHIVES MAIL

This project was carried out in collaboration with the history classes. The
aim was to establish a link between
the historical lessons and transcribe them graphically
through a booklet designed in visual art.

I chose to adopt an original approach to explore
different layout techniques and break the monotony that
history can sometimes represent for young people.

For my final school project, I had to design a complete
collaboration between an artist and a museum. After
extensive research, I discovered Cho Giseok's work,
which particularly appealed to me. I therefore decided
to associate him with the Hoam Museum, a rather atypical
venue that I felt was an appropriate choice given its
recent graphic redesign. I created all the visual
elements required for this collaboration, including the
website, logo, posters, leaflets, etc.

this project earned me a perfect score :)
